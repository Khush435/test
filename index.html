About Us

We are a group of students from DSATM, driven by a passion for making our communities safer. Leveraging our skills in technology, criminal justice, and social advocacy, we are committed to developing a hit-and-run crime reporting website.

Our Aim

By providing a platform for detailed reporting and data collection, we aim to enhance public safety, facilitate law enforcement investigations, and promote community awareness. Our ultimate goal is to reduce the incidence of hit-and-run crimes and ensure justice for victims through improved reporting.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a reliable and user-friendly hit-and-run crime reporting website that empowers victims and witnesses to report incidents swiftly and accurately.
